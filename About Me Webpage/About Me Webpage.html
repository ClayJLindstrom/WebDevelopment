My Interests:

Video Game Design: I am someone who strives to become a Game Designer! I've always been fascinated by design, but when I learned that I could make and design videogames, I was hooked! I've now made two games with the help of some friends, and I plan to make more in the future.

Drawing and Animation: Though I don't want to have a career in animation, I still have a great love for drawing. I made my own art for my games for a while (and they're pretty good, if I can say so myself). I also have a lot of respect for people who work on such art projects.

The Temple: I'm pretty active in my church, the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints, and I love it. Though my favorite place in the church is the Temple, such as the Mt. Timpanogos Utah Temple. There's a feeling of peace there that one doesn't feel anywhere else.
